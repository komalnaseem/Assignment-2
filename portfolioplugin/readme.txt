INSTRUCTIONS FOR PORTFOLIOPLUGIN

This plugin provides facility to the user to record their courses (course title / description / featured image) to their theme. These course titles will be displayed in the widget area of a theme.

Instructions to add/update courses from "Courses" menu item:
============================================================
1.	Hover over the site title on top left corner of the screen and click on "Dashboard".
2.	Find the item "Courses" on the admin menu on the left side (it is in middle of the screen).
3.	Click on "Courses" and then click on "Add New".
4.	Click on "Screen Options" on the top right of corner of the page, check "Featured Image" and "Author" and any other options that you feel is relevant to your site and click on "Screen Options" again. 
5.	To add a course
	i.	Add course title (where it says "Enter title here").
	ii.	If required, add media could be used to add media to the course.
	iii. Add course description (the middle body of the Course). Course description could be formatted by using the appropriate buttons on top of description. Visual or Text tabs could be used as preferred for editing. At the bottom of description, it provides a word count as well. It also provides when this course was last edited.
	iv.	Under the “Author” section (Bottom left of the screen), If required, update the author, by default the current login user will be selected.
	v.	Under the "Featured Image" section (Bottom right of the screen), If required, Add "Featured Image". There is functionality available to load an image from the site or load your own image, edit image size, edit image title and other info about the image.
	vi.	Under the "Publish" section (Top right of the screen), there is functionality available to
		a.	Click on "Save Draft" if you would like to save it and plan to publish later.
		b.	Click on "Preview" if you would like to preview your Course before publishing it.
		c.	Click on "Publish" if you would like to publish your Course.
		d.	All other options can be left as default selections.
	vii.	To add another course click on Add New again and repeat steps i to vi.
6.	If you would like to modify a specific Course, then find that Course in the list by using "Search Course" and "Edit" / "Quick Edit" as required.
7.	If you would like to delete a course then find that course in the list by using "Search Course" and click on "Trash".

Instructions to "Edit" courses from live site:
==============================================

1.	To edit the Course from the live site just click on the "Edit" on the live site and take the necessary actions.


Instructions to setup the widget:
=================================
This widget displays the available courses as a list in the sidebar on all pages except the home page.

1.	Hover over the site title on top left corner of the screen and click on Dashboard.
2.	Find the item "Appearance" on the admin menu on the left side (it is under Courses).
3.	Hover on Appearance and click on Widgets.
4.	Widget screen has two portions (one of the left shows the list of available widgets, one on the right shows the selected widgets in the secondary sidebar).
5.	Find the widget "Course List" and drag it on the right side and place it in the sequence you prefer for it to be shown on your live site.
6.	Once the Course List Widget is added, click on the down arrow on the right side of widget.
7.	By default, the site will display "Course List" as the widget title in the widget area. If you would like to use an alternate title, then enter it in "Title:" otherwise leave it blank and hit save.
8.	Hover on Welcome to My Job Portfolio on top left corner of the screen and click on View Site.
