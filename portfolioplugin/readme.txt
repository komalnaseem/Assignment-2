INSTRUCTIONS FOR PORTFOLIOPLUGIN

This plugin provides facility to the user to record their courses (course title / description) to their theme. These courses title will be dispalyed in the widget area.

Instructions to add/update courses from "Courses" menu item:
============================================================

1. Hover over the site title on top left corner of the screen and click on "Dashboard".
2. Find the item "Courses" on the admin menu on the left side (it is in middle of the screen).
3. Click on "Courses" and then click on "Add New".
4. Click on "Screen Options" on the top right of corner of the page, check "Featured Image" and "Author" and any other options that you feel is relevant to your site and click on "Screen Options" again. 
5. To add a course
	5a. Add course title (where it says "Enter title here").
	5b. Add course description (the middle body of the post)
	5c. If prefer, Add "Feacured Image" (on the right bottom of the screen)
	5d. Click on "Save Draft" if you would like to save it and plan to publish later.
	5e. Click on "Preview" if you would like to preview your post before publishing it.
	5f. Click on "Publish" if you would like to publish your post.
	5g. To add another course click on Add New again and repeat steps 5a to 5f.
6. If you would like to modify a specific post, then find that post in the list by using "Search Post" and "Edit"/"Quick Edit" as required.
7. If you would like to delete a course then find that course in the list by using "Search Post" and click on "Trash".

Instructions to setup the widget:
=================================
This widget displays the available courses as a list in the sidebar on all pages except the home page.

1. Hover over the site title on top left corner of the screen and click on Dashboard.
2. Find the item "Appearence" on the admin menu on the left side (it is under Courses).
3. Hover on Appearence and click on Widgets.
4. Widget screen has two portions (one of the left shows the list of available widgets, one on the right shows the selected widgets in the secondary sidebar).
5. Find the widget "Course List" and drag it on the right side and place it in the sequence you prefer for it to be shown on your live site.
6. Once the Course List Widget is added, click on the down arrow on the right side of widget.
7. By default, the site will display "Course List" as the widget title in the widget area. If you would like to use an alternate title, then enter it in "Title:" other wise leave it blank and hit save.
8. Hover on Welcome to My Job Portfolio on top left corner of the screen and click on View Site.
